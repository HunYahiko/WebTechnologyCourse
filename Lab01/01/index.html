About us

We care about you.

At Raft we know that looks can kill, simplicity is sexy and everyone has a rent to pay. That's why we're bringing you more style for less money. The goal is to create a space where style is affordable. We've basically gone ahead and made outfitting your home as easy as ordering a pizza.

The Key to Raft

Here's the skinny: we believe simple is sophisticated. We love coffee table books with big pictures and little words. A backless barstool is just sexy. It's called a sofa not a couch, you potato! We're digging pastels, sherpa, and Snoopy right now—tomorrow's a new day. We think solids trump plaids (but to each their own). We believe you can't buy taste, but you can still have style. Kill them with kindness - but knock 'em out with one 'wow' piece. We're East Coasters by blood with a dash of mad West Coast flavor. We say if you're going to talk trash you better back it up. We sleep and sit on the same beds and sofas that we sell. You should always have fresh flowers in your house. Paisley, pinstripes and gingham are timeless. Rock the boat but wear a lifejacket.
